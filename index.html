Welcome to dfbrowse!


I know this looks like no browser you've used before, but it's designed to get us the tools we need when LISD won't.


To get started, enter a URL in the box and press submit.

User guide:

Alt-LeftArrow takes you back now, y'all

Alt-RightArrow takes you forward

CTRL-R reloads your current page

Credit goes to the Electron team for making this possible, and Travis Weir for putting this together.
